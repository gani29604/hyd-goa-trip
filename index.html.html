Afternoon–Night Drive Heads Up
Starting 2–3 PM means the Hubli–Ankola ghat section (~60 km of hairpin bends) will be driven around midnight. Stay sharp, take short breaks, avoid driving drowsy. The ghats are doable at night — just go slower than usual.

~1:30 AM
Western Ghats at night — speed drops to 30–40 km/h. Winding hairpin bends, no streetlights, occasional fog. Drive slowly and carefully. Stunning but needs full focus. No overtaking on blind curves.

💡 Important Tips
🌙
Night Drive Safety
Take turns driving if possible. Stop and rest if anyone feels drowsy — don't push through.
⛽
Half-Tank Rule
Never let fuel drop below half. Night pumps may be closed in smaller towns.
📱
Offline Maps
Download Hubli–Goa offline in Google Maps. Signal drops heavily in the ghats at night.
💳
Payments
Carry cash + keep FASTag recharged. Most NH tolls are FASTag-only now.
🌀
Ghat at Night
After Ankola, no streetlights. Use high beam wisely, watch for animals crossing, no overtaking.
🎒
Essentials
Water, snacks, power bank, torch. Tire inflator/puncture kit is a lifesaver on night drives.
